   - "Offset More" and "View Details" actions

   - Icon: Sun (green)
   - Renewable energy percentage (73%)
   - Energy mix progress bar

   - Individual progress bars for each source
   - Renewable vs Grid energy comparison
   - "Energy Report" and "Add Sources" actions
